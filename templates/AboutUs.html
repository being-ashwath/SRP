Sugarisk:About Us

Considering today's lifestyle, it is good to diagnosis a disease earlier so that preventions can be taken to save the person. One common disease that can be left undiagnosed due to carelessness is diabetes. Since this is considered as an underdiagnosed disease (the symptoms aren't shown in the early stages) people might be careless and wouldn't know that they have been sick. Also people don't really spend time to do regular health checkups to make sure their body is working perfectly fine. Keeping all these facts into consideration we have developed this app, such that it would be helpful just as a first level of diagnosing that too at home. It is easily accessible to everyone just by knowing the basic information about them. Hence with the launch of this app we believe that it will be easy for the people to atleast have an idea if they are prone to diabetes.
