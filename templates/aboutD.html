Sugarisk:Diabetes

Diabetes is a disease that occurs when your blood glucose, also called blood sugar, is too high. It is one of the deadliest diseases in the world. It is not only a disease but also creator of different kinds of diseases like heart attack, blindness etc.

Blood Glucose

A blood sugar level of less than 140 mg/dL is considered normal. A result between 140 mg/dL and 199 mg/dL is considered prediabetes and 200mg/dL or higher is considered diabetes.

Blood Pressure

Ideal blood pressure is considered to be between 90/60mmHg and 120/80mmHg. high blood pressure is considered to be 140/90mmHg or higher. low blood pressure is considered to be 90/60mmHg or lower.

Body Mass Index

Being “overweight” (BMI of 25-29.9), or affected by obesity (BMI of 30-39.9) or severe obesity (BMI of 40 or greater), greatly increases your risk of developing diabetes.

Diabetes Pedigree Function

It provides information about diabetes history in relatives and genetic relationship of those relatives with patients. Higher Pedigree Function means patient is more likely to have diabetes.

Insulin

Insulin is needed to move blood sugar into cells throughout the body. Diabetes occurs when insulin-producing cells are damaged or destroyed and stop producing insulin. Normal Insulin Level 16-166 mIU/L.

Skin Thickness

A value used to estimate body fat. Normal Triceps SkinFold Thickness is 23mm. Higher thickness leads to obesity and chances of diabetes increases.
